About Me

Hello and welcome to my profile page.

My name is Brian Wong. I was born in Seattle and went to school at the University of Washington.

During my studies at the University of Washington, I worked as a library assistant assigned to the Music Library. I was a member of BITS (Business Information Technology Society) and the IMA Archery Club. My areas of concentration were Finance/Information System from the Michael G Foster Business School and Economics from the school of Arts and Sciences. I also took a course abroad in Shanghai, China to observe the rapid ascent of the modern Chinese economy.

My hobbies include baseball, photography, commercial aviation, traveling. I am a support of the Seattle Mariners and have been attending games as a child since the Kingdome.

My past work experiences have spanned customer service, retail sales, IT outsourcing, and project management.

I've lived abroad in Beijing, China for several years in order to enhance my Mandarin skillsets and to have a personal viewpoint of the rapid development of the Chinese economy.

Fun Fact: I've been to the tourist basecamp of Mount Everest in Tibet, the highest border crossing in the world at Khunjerab Pass (China-Pakistan border), and been atop the sand dunes of Mongolia at Khongoryn Els.
